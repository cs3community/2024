What is the IOP?

ScienceMesh is the Federated Science Cloud Mesh that connects existing and heterogenous sites in a transparent way. Is very important to ensure inter-operability that the sites are able to talk to each other independenlty of the technology stack. For such reason, the IOP is a compoent to be deployed in each site to ensure compatibilty across sites and being able to join the mesh.
